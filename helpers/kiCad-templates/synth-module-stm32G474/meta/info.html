Euro Rack Module with a basic STM32 G474

Template with a configured STM32 G474. The STM32 G474 is a 32-bit ARM Cortex-M4 microcontroller with 128KB of RAM and 512KB of Flash.

Datasheet: https://www.lcsc.com/datasheet/lcsc_datasheet_2304140030_STMicroelectronics-STM32G474RBT3_C1235414.pdf
